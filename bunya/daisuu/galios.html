\[n : Z_{\geq5}\]

\[t_1,\cdots,t_n:{\bf Q}上代数的独立な元 \]

\[f(X) = X^n+t_1X^{n-1}+\cdots+t_n\]

\[f(X)はべき根によって解くことはできない\]

\[\alpha_1,\cdots,\alpha_n:f(X) = (X-\alpha_1)\cdots(X-\alpha_n)\]
\[\]
\[K = {\bf Q} (t_1,\cdots,t_n)\]
\[\]
\[L = {\bf Q} (\alpha_1,\cdots,\alpha_n)\]
\[\]
\[L^{\prime} = {\bf Q} (X_1,\cdots,X_n)\]
\[\]
\[s_1,\cdots,s_n:X_1,\cdots,X_nの基本対象式\]
\[\]
\[g(X) = X^n+s_1X^{n-1}+\cdots+s_n\]
